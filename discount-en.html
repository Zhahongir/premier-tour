Tour program

1

DAY

Meeting. Transfer, hotel accommodation.
Evening beautiful photo walk along the beautiful streets of the old city in Tbilisi

2

DAY

Breakfast in the hotel and we are going to Kakheti - cradleland of wine. We will taste wine, cognac and Chacha at the wineries and private graperies. We will visit the city of love Sighnaghi, where the famous artist Pirosmani lived, who has sold his house for love and he has bought a million scarlet roses for his ladylove. (It's very interesting). We will see with our own eyes the Alazani Valley and the monastery of St. Nino. We will have dinner at a mountain restaurant and of course supper with songs in our hotel.

3

DAY

Breakfast in the hotel and we are going to the world famous ski resort Bakuriani - this is Courchevel in Georgia. Snow-capped mountain peaks, beautiful landscapes, and of course pistes and slides. Everyone will find entertainment to his liking in Bakuriani, from skiing and snowboarding to horse-riding expedition.

There are a lot of pistes (slopes) on the territory of the resort, ranging from child's to professional. On the spot You can rent outfitting, equipment, snowracers, snowmobiles and a lot more. There are instructors' services for beginners.

A lot of positive emotions and adrenalin are guaranteed! Of course, we will bring a lot of cool pictures! On return to Tbilisi evening sauna with great beer!)

4

DAY

Mtskheta — the first capital of Georgia, the confluence of the two rivers, we will visit Jvari monastery.
We will visit the Jvari Monastery and Svetitskhoveli Cathedral, in which is kept the greatest shrine of the Christian world - the Chiton of our Lord Jesus Christ

5

DAY

Breakfast in the hotel. Then we will go to the famous resort Borjomi, taste real water from the springs, take photos in a very beautiful park, swim in the mineral springs, climb the funicular, with picturesque views of the picturesque nature and mountains of Borjomi resort.

We will go to Akhaltsikhe city, where the most beautiful Akhaltsikhe (Rabati) Castle of the XVI-XVIII centuries is located, delve into the atmosphere of the Middle Ages, take a lot of cool photos and have a great time. Next we will visit Vardzia city, which is one of the most amazing sights of Samtskhe-Javakheti and all of Georgia. This is a city and a monastery in a cave, one of the symbols of Georgia along with Mtskheta, Kazbegi and Mestia. Monks still live there. You will simply fall in love with these places, believe me!

6

DAY

Breakfast in the hotel. Leisure time. Transfer.

Goodbye Georgia - See you again!
