Useful information

  • We are pleased to inform you that tour reservations is carried out without prepayment in Premier Tour!

    To book a tour, you must send copies of paid air tickets for each guest, in any convenient way for you: Email, WhatsApp, Viber, Telegram. Having previously agreed all the details and free dates of arrival with our managers.

  • It is easy to get to Georgia from any part of the world

    There are several variants of arrival from Russian into Georgia. We will tell you about the simplest variants!

    First variant - via Minsk (Belarus)
    The air travel is carried out from any city of Russia with a transit transfer in Minsk. Moscow — Minsk — Tbilisi.

    Second variant via Yerevan (Armenia)
    Direct flights from Russia to Armenia are carried out from many cities: Moscow, Saint-Petersburg, Saratov, Kaluga, Mineralnye Vody, Rostov-on-Don, Ufa, Krasnodar, Sochi, Volgograd, Yekaterinburg, Voronezh, Samara, Nizhny Novgorod, Novosibirsk.

    Premier Tour company provides transfer from Yerevan airport to Tbilisi on comfortable transport. Travel time is 4-5 hours, which fly away invisibly, admiring the landscape scene outside the window.

    When crossing the Armenia-Georgia border, a PCR test is required, made no later than 72 hours at the time of crossing the border. PCR test is required as for vaccinated so for non-vaccinated tourists when crossing the land border! A VISA TO ENTRANCE TO THE COUNTRY IS NOT REQUIRED!

    Third variant - Transit air travel via Yerevan
    In this case, you need to choose air tickets Moscow — Yerevan — Tbilisi.

    Direct flights are made to the airports of Tbilisi, Kutaisi, Batumi from the CIS countries: Belarus, Ukraine, Kazakhstan, Armenia, as well as European countries

  • To entrance to Georgia, the guests of the country must present a vaccination passport or a PCR test, made no later than 72 hours at the time of arrival.

    PCR test is required as for vaccinated so for non-vaccinated tourists when crossing the land border!

  • Georgia is a country of mountains. The Caucasus Range provides soft weather, comfortable for recreation on the whole territory of the country. There is subtropical climate in the west and moderate climate in the central and eastern parts of Georgia.

    Summer in Georgia is hot and humid, while winter is moderately cold. Medium temperature of January ranges from - 2 °С (Kolkheti) to + 3 °С; from + 23 to + 26 ° С in August. While the northern mountain peaks are permanently snow-capped, the western coast of the Black Sea enjoys pleasant weather all the year.
